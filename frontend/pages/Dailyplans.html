FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know about your Daily Box

What if I miss a delivery? ↓
If you're not home, we'll leave the box at your door or with a neighbour. You can also reschedule a delivery up to 10 PM the night before via the app or by contacting us directly.
Why only 1 seed item per day? ↓
Seeds are nutritionally dense and consuming too many types together can be heavy on digestion. We include exactly one seed item per box, rotating daily — so you get the full benefits without overloading.
Can I switch my plan mid-month? ↓
Yes. You can switch your plan category at any time. The change takes effect from the next billing cycle. You can also change your delivery slot anytime for free.
How does the 7-day rotation work exactly? ↓
We have 7 unique daily combinations. Day 1 differs from Day 2, all the way to Day 7. On Day 8 your box matches Day 1 again — and repeats throughout the month, ensuring variety every day.
Can I pause deliveries when I'm travelling? ↓
Yes. You can pause for up to 15 days per month. Paused days are automatically extended to the next month — you never lose a delivery you paid for.
Are the item weights consistent every day? ↓
Each item is portioned to a standard serve size — typically 30–50g per item. Total box weight varies slightly day to day but is always aligned with your plan's nutritional goals.
