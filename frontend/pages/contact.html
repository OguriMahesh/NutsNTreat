Frequently Asked Questions
Quick answers to common questions
How long does delivery take?
Standard delivery takes 3–5 business days. Express delivery (1–2 days) is available for select pin codes in metro cities.
What is your return policy?
We accept returns within 7 days of delivery for damaged or incorrect items. Perishable items are non-returnable once opened.
Are products 100% natural?
Yes! All our products are sourced directly from farms, with no artificial preservatives, colours, or flavours added.
Do you offer bulk / corporate orders?
Absolutely! We offer special pricing for bulk orders above ₹5,000. Contact us with your requirements for a custom quote.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept UPI, Credit/Debit Cards, Net Banking, and Cash on Delivery (COD) for orders up to ₹10,000.
Do you offer gift packaging?
Yes! Premium gift boxes are available at checkout for special occasions like Diwali, birthdays, and corporate gifting.
